### Monthly & Yearly Billing

You can choose the billing cycle that works best for you using the toggle at the top of the pricing section.
- **Monthly:** Pay on a month-to-month basis for maximum flexibility.
- **Yearly:** Pay for a full year upfront and receive a significant discount (up to 35% off).

You can also toggle between **INR (₹)** and **USD ($)** to see pricing in your preferred currency.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the contact system work in AdsEra?">
    With AdsEra, you choose a plan that matches your contact volume. You can then send unlimited emails to those contacts until your plan expires.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How can I improve my email open rates?">
    Craft compelling subject lines that are relevant and personalized. Consider using emojis, questions, or creating a sense of urgency to encourage opens.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I authenticate a domain to improve email deliverability?">
    Domains are authenticated using protocols like SPF, DKIM, and DMARC. In AdsEra, configuring DMARC and DKIM records is mandatory to add a domain, which helps protect your sender reputation and improve deliverability.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
