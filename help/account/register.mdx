Welcome to AdsEra! Creating an account is a simple, multi-step process. You can either register with your **email and password** or use your **Google account** for a faster setup.


**Ready to create your account? [Go to the AdsEra Registration Page &rarr;](https://app.adsera.in/auth/register)**

Follow the guide below that matches your preferred method.

---

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Email & Password Signup">

This is the standard method for creating an account. It involves filling out a form, verifying your email with a one-time password (OTP), and selecting a plan.

### Step 1: Fill Out the Registration Form

First, you'll need to provide your personal and account details.

![AdsEra Registration Form Screenshot](https://sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/whatsera/bef42dc6-10ad-41be-8d5d-a935f3013511_register_page.png)

<Steps>
    <Step title="Enter Personal Details">
        Fill in your **First name** and **Last name**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Account Credentials">
        Provide a valid **Email address** and create a secure **Password**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Contact Information">
        Select your **Country** from the dropdown list and enter your **Phone number**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Submit the Form">
        After filling in all fields, click the **Register** button to proceed.
    </Step>
</Steps>

---

### Step 2: Verify Your Email Address

To ensure your email is valid, we'll send you a verification code.

![AdsEra OTP Verification Screenshot](https://sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/whatsera/e446a7a4-b455-4510-be75-98b45957d4c6_otp_verification_page.png)

<Steps>
    <Step title="Check Your Email">
        Look for an email from AdsEra containing a 6-digit one-time password (OTP). If you don't see it, check your spam folder.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter the OTP">
        Type the 6-digit code into the input fields on the verification page.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Verify Your Account">
        Click the **Verify** button. If the code is correct, your account will be successfully created.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you haven't received the code, you can use the **resend OTP** button.
</Info>

---

### Step 3: Select Your Plan

After successful verification, you'll be logged in and directed to the **Plans & Subscriptions** page.

![AdsEra Plan Selection Screenshot](https://sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/whatsera/f4d9c4d8-f0a0-4ed4-923b-f961dafbbc7c_plan_page.png)

Your account is automatically placed on the **Free plan**, which allows you to start exploring AdsEra's features immediately. You can choose to upgrade to a paid plan at any time. Your registration is now complete!

</Tab>

<Tab title="Sign-up with Google">

This method allows you to create an account using your existing Google profile, which simplifies the initial steps.

### Step 1: Authenticate with Google

Begin by selecting the Google sign-up option on the registration page.

1.  Click the **Sign-up with Google** button.
2.  A pop-up window will appear. Choose the Google account you wish to use.
3.  Grant the necessary permissions to link your Google account with AdsEra.

---

### Step 2: Provide Additional Details

After authenticating with Google, we just need a few more details to complete your profile.

![AdsEra Additional Details Form Screenshot](https://sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/whatsera/75ab8b65-2feb-45fa-8070-d84a1670bf86_phone_verification_page.png)

<Steps>
    <Step title="Enter Company and Phone">
        Fill in your **Company name** and **Phone number**. Your country should be pre-selected.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Save Your Information">
        Click the **Save** button to finalize your account details.
    </Step>
</Steps>

---

### Step 3: Select Your Plan

Just like with the email sign-up, you'll be directed to the **Plans & Subscriptions** page after saving your details.

![AdsEra Plan Selection Screenshot](https://sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/whatsera/f4d9c4d8-f0a0-4ed4-923b-f961dafbbc7c_plan_page.png)

Your account is automatically started on the **Free plan**. You can begin using the platform right away or explore options to upgrade. Your registration is now complete!

</Tab>
</Tabs>
