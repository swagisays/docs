To access your AdsEra dashboard, you need to sign in to your account. The login page provides two methods: using your registered email and password or signing in with your Google account.

**Login to your account? [Go to the AdsEra Login Page &rarr;](https://app.adsera.in/auth/login)**

![A screenshot of the AdsEra login page.](https://sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/whatsera/daec3e8f-7cc9-4c73-a30c-37ee27ea2d61_adsera_login_page.png)

---

## Standard Login

This is the primary method for signing into your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email Address">
    In the **Email address** field, type the email you used to register your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Password">
    In the **Password** field, type your secret password. You can click the eye icon to show or hide the characters you're typing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Login">
    Click the **Login** button to be redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Sign in with Google

For quick access, you can authenticate using your existing Google account.

1.  Below the main login form, click the **Sign-in with Google** button.
2.  A pop-up window will appear, prompting you to choose a Google account.
3.  Select the desired account and follow the authentication steps.
4.  Upon successful authentication, you will be redirected to the AdsEra dashboard.
