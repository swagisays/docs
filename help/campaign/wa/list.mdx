**Want to Manage WhatsApp Campaigns? [Go to the AdsEra WhatsApp Campaign Page &rarr;](https://app.adsera.in/dashboard/campaign/whatsapp/list)**

The WhatsApp Campaign List is your command center for managing all your WhatsApp marketing efforts. This page provides a real-time snapshot of all your campaigns, allowing you to quickly assess performance, create new campaigns, and manage existing ones with ease.

![The WhatsApp Campaign List page.](https://sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/whatsera/037e00ca-9d02-416c-85ca-c976907d2209_camp_wa_list.png)

### Navigating to Your WhatsApp Campaigns
Accessing your list is simple:
<Steps>
    <Step title="Select Campaigns">From your AdsEra Dashboard, click on **Campaigns** in the left-hand navigation bar.</Step>
    <Step title="Choose WhatsApp">From the campaign submenu, select **WhatsApp** to view your list.</Step>
</Steps>

## Anatomy of a WhatsApp Campaign Card

Each card on the list represents an individual WhatsApp campaign and provides key information at a glance. Here's a detailed breakdown:

| Section | Field / Metric | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Header** | **Campaign Name** | The internal name you assigned to your campaign for easy identification. |
| | **Sent On / Last Updated** | The date and time the campaign was sent. For drafts, this shows the last time it was modified. |
| | **Status Tag** | A color-coded tag indicating the campaign's current status: `DRAFT` or `SENT`. |
| **Details** | **Channel** | The specific WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) number used to send the campaign. |
| | **Template** | The name of the pre-approved WhatsApp Message Template used for the campaign. |
| **Metrics** | **Recipients** | The total number of unique contacts the campaign was sent to. |
| | **Delivers** | The number of messages successfully delivered to the recipients' devices. |
| | **Read**| The number of recipients who have read the message (indicated by blue ticks). |
| | **Failed**| The number of messages that failed to deliver. |

## Searching and Filtering Your Campaigns

To help you find specific campaigns quickly, you have powerful filtering options at the top of the page:

- **Date Range Filters:** Use the **Start** and **End** date pickers to narrow down your list to campaigns sent within a specific period.
- **Search Bar:** Type into the **Search...** field to find campaigns by their name.
